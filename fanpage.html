Bio

Irish pop vocal group Westlife has released eleven studio albums, two extended plays, sixteen video albums, one thve album, three karaoke albums, three box sets, forty-one singles, twenty-one promotional singles, nine compilation albums and fifty-four music videos.
Formed on 3 July 1998, the group was made up of the folllowing singers:

  • Nicky Byrne
  • Kian Egan
  • Mark Feehily
  • Shane Filan
  • Brian McFadden (who quit the band on 9 March 2004)
